Maize
Maize is a cereal crop that is grown widely throughout the world in a range of agroecological environments. More maize is produced, by weight, than any other grain, and almost every country on Earth cultivates maize commercially for a variety of uses.
Types of Maize
There are many different types of maize, including dent corn, flint corn, and sweet corn.
Uses of Maize
Maize can be used in a variety of ways, including in food products, animal feed, and industrial products.
